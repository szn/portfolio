Hi!

During last 5 years I had been working as a client, a designer and and as a person between this two before. The last one teached me the most. Every time when I do something I'm still learning how not to fulfill my own ambition and that "done" is better than "the best". I think it is the most hardest topic at each of my jobs. When I had managed the biggest project in my work life, Polish Guitar Academy, every year I had the restrictive deadline – the date when the Festival start. In the perspective of 2017 it was great lesson of how you can do something just good, but the best as you can at that time.

If somebody ask me, what my cultural studies gave my in my current work - I would say it was the best way to learn a mix of social, cognitive and psychology science that helps me to better understand "user experiences" and user's perception (Maybe that’s why I’m not an artist.). Nowadays I want to get more technical skills to better understand people with I am working with and know each part of product design process.
